A Message from Our Head Pastor

Beloved brothers and sisters in the Church of Almighty God of Heaven,

Grace and peace be multiplied unto you. It is with a heart full of gratitude and humility that I extend this message to each of you. As we gather in faith, whether in spirit or in person, let us remember the divine purpose that unites us and the sacred calling we share.

In these times, we are constantly reminded of the enduring presence and boundless love of Almighty God. His wisdom guides us, His strength sustains us, and His light illuminates our path forward. Let us continue to seek His divine will in all our endeavors, trusting in His perfect plan for our lives and for His kingdom.

I encourage each of you to grow deeper in your personal walk with God, to nourish your spirits through prayer and meditation, and to uphold the virtues of love, compassion, and understanding in your daily interactions. Let our lives be a testament to the transformative power of His grace.

May our community continue to be a beacon of hope, a sanctuary of truth, and a family united by unwavering faith. Let us lift one another up, share in each other's joys and burdens, and always strive to embody the teachings that bring us closer to our Heavenly Father.

May Almighty God of Heaven bless you abundantly, fill your hearts with His peace, and grant you renewed strength and purpose.

In His unfailing love,

Ptr. Amador Acebuche
